Our Top Reporters

Rahul Rawat

With a career spanning two decades in the media industry, he stands tall with our channel since the channel's inception in 2004. His professional journey has been multifarious, he has covered several news-making events including consecutive Lok Sabha elections - 2009, 2014 and 2019. In his divergent reportage, he has delivered exclusive ground reports, including the 26/11 Mumbai terror attacks, and Kashmir floods. He currently anchors ‘Mann ki baat with Rahul’ - a special debate show at 6 pm, every day. An engineer by profession, Rahul also dabbled in the world of theatre. However, he ultimately found his true calling in the world of journalism.

Sakshi

Sakshi, among the latest entrants in our channel, possesses a inquisitive and sarcastic approach towards news that matter. She started her career from Local Cable Network DIGI in the year 2010 Jodhpur and never looked back. She has been the face of news channels like India News , News Express and Focus News. While her education relates to Economics and History, she has a keen interest in areas of humanities and sports.
